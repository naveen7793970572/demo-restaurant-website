Our Heritage

Welcome to Sri Sai Tiffins, where every dish tells a story of tradition, passion, and culinary excellence. Located in the heart of Vijayawada, we have been serving authentic South Indian delicacies since 1990.

Our recipes have been passed down through generations, using only the finest ingredients and hand-ground spices to ensure every bite transports you to the warmth of a true South Indian home.
